Yekyel means Village of job. Every work, we do these days is purely based on the digital system. With arising vying for commercial and employment, the general idea of online jobs and accessing the availability of jobs in the market via the digitalized system is the purpose of the Yokyel. This way, the job seekers will be able to make their work easier in getting jobs and reduce their expenditure. The organizations will be able to get their expected candidates and reduce their expenditure.
